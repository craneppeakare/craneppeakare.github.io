[Review] Ultralearning by Scott H. Young

Finally my long awaited review of the book “Ultralearning” by Scott H. Young. I have been putting this off for far too long. This will not be a summary of the book as I have already done so in another post which you can find here. As someone who loves constantly learning new hobbies I thought this book would be right up my alley for leveling up my abilities.

The book opens itself well by defining the demographic who would benefit the most from this book. It takes itself very seriously as a time intensive process to pursue anything you want. It doesn’t aim to show you how to learn any particular skill but it shows you principles which you can use and apply depending on the type of skill.

One thing to note is that I have personally used all of these principles in at least some form or another in my daily life. Which I guess means I am on the right path and I agree are highly effective. Going into the book I was expecting something insightful to revolutionize the way I learn but it’s more a standard list of strategies that one can employ to structure your learning in the most time effective way.
